Protection First

Your Security is Our Highest Priority

We employ military-grade encryption and advanced security protocols to ensure your data remains protected at all times.

End-to-End Encryption

All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is protected with AES-256 encryption. Your personal information, applications, and support tickets are encrypted both in transit and at rest.

Privacy Protection

We never sell or share your data with third parties. Your Discord and Uplay information is stored securely and only accessible to authorized staff members with proper authentication.

Secure Infrastructure

Our database uses JSONBin.io with master key authentication and secure HTTPS connections. Regular security audits and monitoring ensure your data stays safe from unauthorized access.

Security Measures in Place

  • HTTPS Everywhere: All connections use TLS 1.3 encryption protocols
  • Secure Authentication: Staff portal protected with hashed credentials and session management
  • Data Minimization: We only collect essential information needed for team applications
  • Regular Backups: Automated data backups to prevent information loss
  • Access Controls: Role-based permissions ensure staff only access necessary data
